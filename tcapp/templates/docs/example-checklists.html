Checklists (Example)

There are three major steps in the LIHTC tenant certification process before of a household moves in. You will want to include in your overall move-in manual the required bullet check points to comply with the LIHTC Tax Credit program.

Application

At the application stage, you will want to make sure all prospective residents are listed in the application.

  • Children
  • Marital status

Verification

At the verification stage, you will want to make sure you accounted for all sources of income and assets.

  • Minimum of a 2 year housing history
  • Applicants listed match the tenants occupying the unit
  • Application indicates all sources of income and assets from wages, pensions, IRA’s, 401k’s, etc.
  • Real Estate
  • Full-Time Student Households
  • All release of information (VOE, etc.) forms have been signed.

Review credit reports for the following:

  • Mortgages/short sales/foreclosures
  • Past and current Employment

3 Consecutive Months of paystubs and 3 method of W2 income calculations are required. Review paystubs for:

  • Additional bank accounts, 401K/retirement accounts and Life Insurance that was not already disclosed.

Review Bank Statements for:

  • Additional undisclosed income or assets.

Taxes are required for all Self Employment applicants, be sure to review tax returns for:

  • Any additional undisclosed income or assets.

Certification

  • On day of Move-In, prior to the TIC being signed on the effective date, confirm with the applicant if there has not been any changes in the House Holds income or assets since their certification appointment.
  • No “stale” dated documentation. Confirm that documents that are submitted are not older than 120 days from certification effective date. VOD’s, paystubs, VOE’s, etc..
